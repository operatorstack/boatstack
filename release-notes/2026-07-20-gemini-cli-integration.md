### Integrate Gemini CLI into Boatstack as a first-class supported adapter

Gemini CLI is now fully supported as a first-class adapter for Boatstack. The local projector automatically packages and brands the `gemini.yaml` agent configuration, and exports the full suite of interactive, visible `boatstack-` skills under `.gemini/skills/` to provide a seamless, native conversational delivery and repair experience within the Gemini CLI environment.
